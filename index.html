This system detects hazardous LPG leaks, smoke, and fire using sensors connected to a microcontroller. Data is transmitted over IoT platforms to notify users and trigger alarms or automatic responses such as activating exhaust fans or fire sprinklers.

Step 1: Sensors (MQ-2/MQ-5, flame sensor) detect LPG, gas, or fire presence.
Step 2: Microcontroller (ESP32/Arduino) processes sensor data.
Step 3: If danger is detected, a buzzer activates and a signal is sent to the cloud.
Step 4: Users receive real-time alerts via smartphone app or dashboard.
Step 5: Optional: Robotic arm or system triggers extinguishing system.
